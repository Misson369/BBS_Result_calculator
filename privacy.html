Third-party services

We do not include tracking scripts or analytics in the calculator page by default. If third-party widgets are added (for example comment systems or feedback tools), they may collect and store information according to their own policies.

Cookies

This site does not use cookies for the calculator purpose. If cookie banners or consent tools appear, they are to manage third-party widgets (if added).

Changes to this Policy
